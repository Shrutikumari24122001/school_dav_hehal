D.A.V SCHOOL OVERVIEW
Best School for primary as well as Secondary Education
Published Date:18-oct-2021
DAYANAND ANGLO VEDIC(D.A.V) is established by Swami Dayanand Saraswati.The school provide best education to the primary as well as Secondary classes.There are separate buildings for junior and senior classes.Each building has a well-organised library,playground,music room,sports room,computer lab,etc.The authority ensures that the students maintain the discipline.
The School provide best education to the students
Published Date:25-oct-2021
The purpose of the teachers of our school is not to provide education only for learning from books and passing some examinations.In addition to book learning,teaches motivate students to find a well balanced a properly developed mind and healthy body such that students will turn into a worthy member of society. According to the teachers,education is like a lamp in a dark place.Just as the approach of a lamp the accumulated darkness of the house is dispelled in a moment,similarly,the darkness of ignorance is driven away by true educatin.A man becomes conscious of his powers and responsibilities at the same time.
